How to use the Audio Player

The basic functions of the program are unlocked upon successful sign in. Simply register your account. An message will confirm or deny the status of your registrationa attempt. Sign in using the details entered during registration. Afterwards, all buttons will become enabled.

Playing Audio:

To play audio, you must create a playlist first. Afterwards, select your desired playlist and click Add Song. The file chooser is configured to load .wav files, so seek out audio files of that extension for this program. Once it has been added, you can press the play button to start playback. The various other button icons represent the basic, self-explanatory functionalities of audio playing: pause, resume, stop, next and previous.

Saving:

To save your playlists and songs, click the save button in the bottom left corner. This will save all your current playlists and songs into a csv file located in the data directory, under users, under your username. Next time you sign in, the program will load these playlists into the your playlist list so that you can play your audio files again.
